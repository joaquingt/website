About Us

OJIS, Inc. has been providing our community with exceptional services for over 25 years. Our office is conveniently located in Covina, CA on the Southeast corner of Grand Ave. and Cienega Ave.

Our office caters to individual clients living throughout Southern California. We use state-of-the-art client management software, which allows us to safely process and manage our clientele's information. Our experienced staff answers all client inquiries and we strive in providing our existing and prospective clients with exceptional customer service.

Our most important goal is to give an efficient, friendly and direct service to each individual client. Our exemplary service to our clients speaks for itself as 90% of our new clients are direct referral from prior or existing clients who have been satisfied with our service.

We greatly appreciate our client's loyalty and trust, therefore, we proudly continue to advance our office programs and software. We have been serving the community for over 25 years and we hope to continue doing so for many years to come.
